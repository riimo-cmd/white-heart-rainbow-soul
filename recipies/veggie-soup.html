Ingrediants:
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 tomatos, diced
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, corn, or small frozen vegetable of your choice
  • two handfuls of spinach or dark leafy green of your choice
  • 1 tbsp of white wine vinigar
  • olive oil to taste
  • salt to taste
Steps:
  • add onion and carrot to a quart of boiling water and let cook for 5 minutes
  • add tomato and allow soup to cook until the carrots are tender
  • once carrot is cooked, add large frozen vegetables (broccoli/cauliflower/green beans) and leafy greens, continue to cook for one minute
  • salt to taste and add vinigar
  • add small frozen vegetables (carrots/corn/lima beans) and take off heat
  • add olive oil and serve :D
